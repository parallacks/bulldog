Building your family legacy, one ancestor at a time. We research, verify, and compile your complete family history.

What is Family Tree Construction?

Family Tree Construction is the process of researching and compiling a comprehensive genealogical chart that outlines your ancestry. It involves:

  • Identifying direct ancestors (parents, grandparents, great-grandparents, etc.) and, where possible, collateral lines (aunts, uncles, cousins).
  • Verifying connections with primary and secondary source documentation.
  • Gathering key biographical details for each individual, such as birth, marriage, and death dates and locations.
  • Organizing the information into a clear, well-documented family tree format.
  • Providing context about your ancestors' lives and the historical periods they lived in.

Whether you're just starting or looking to expand on existing research, we can help you build an accurate and detailed family tree.

Our Family Tree Construction Approach

At Brick Wall Bulldog, we take a meticulous and thorough approach to building your family tree:

Initial Assessment & Goal Setting

We start by discussing your current knowledge, any previous research, and your specific goals for the family tree project.

Systematic Record Searching

We systematically search a wide array of records, including vital records, census data, immigration papers, land records, and more.

Source Verification & Analysis

Every piece of information is carefully evaluated and cross-referenced to ensure accuracy and resolve any discrepancies.

Detailed Reporting

You'll receive a comprehensive report including the family tree chart, copies of key documents, and detailed source citations.

Our goal is to provide you with a family tree that is not only extensive but also reliably documented and rich with historical detail.
