How It Works

1

Initial Consultation

We start with a free consultation to discuss what you know, what you want to know, and how we can help.

2

Research Plan

If you decide to move forward, we will develop a customized research plan, an estimated timeline, and an expected budget. You will know exactly what to expect.

3

We Get to Work

We start digging into archives, databases, and specialized resources. We piece together all the evidence to solve your genealogical puzzle.

4

Progress Updates

You will receive regular updates with as much or as little information as you want, so that you can follow along with our discoveries or wait for the final product.

5

Comprehensive Results

You will receive the results in the form you want- a formal write-up or just all the information for you to move forward on your own.
