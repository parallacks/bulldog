Discover your complete family story. We trace your lineage, explore your ethnic heritage, and connect you to your ancestors across generations and continents.

Explore the Full Tapestry of Your Heritage

Ancestry research goes beyond solving a single problem; it's about painting a complete picture of your family's past. Our comprehensive research services aim to:

  • Trace Multiple Family Lines: Follow your paternal and maternal lines back through as many generations as records allow.
  • Uncover Ethnic Origins: Explore the diverse geographic and cultural roots that make up your unique heritage.
  • Map Migration Patterns: Discover when and why your ancestors moved from one place to another.
  • Understand Historical Context: Learn about the lives your ancestors lived within the social, economic, and historical events of their time.
  • Identify Occupations and Lifestyles: Gain insights into how your ancestors lived, worked, and contributed to their communities.
  • Connect with Living Relatives: Potentially identify and connect with distant cousins who share your heritage.

We help you build a rich, detailed understanding of who your ancestors were and the journey that led to you.

Our Ancestry Research Methodology

We combine thorough research techniques with a passion for storytelling to bring your ancestry to life:

Foundation Building

We start with what you know, verify existing information, and build a solid foundation for deeper research.

Systematic Generational Research

Working backwards one generation at a time, we meticulously search for records for each ancestor.

Broad Record Collection

We utilize a wide range of sources, including census, vital, land, probate, military, immigration, and church records, as well as newspapers and local histories.

DNA Integration (Optional)

If you've taken a DNA test, we can incorporate those results to guide research, confirm findings, and uncover new avenues.

Our commitment is to provide you with well-documented, accurate, and engaging insights into your family's complete history.

Ancestry Journeys We've Uncovered

Tracing a Multi-Ethnic Heritage

Maria knew she had diverse roots but wanted to understand the specific origins of her various family lines in Europe and the Americas.

Our Research Unveiled:

We successfully traced four main ancestral lines back to Ireland, Germany, colonial New England, and French Canada, providing documentation and stories for each branch, and explaining their migration paths to the United States.

Reconnecting with Immigrant Roots

John's great-grandparents immigrated in the late 19th century, but the family knew little about their lives before arriving in America or their specific places of origin.

Our Research Unveiled:

We identified their villages in Poland and Sicily, located their passenger arrival records, and found records of their parents and siblings in their home countries, providing a richer understanding of their immigrant experience.
