Family History Books

Transform your research into a lasting legacy. We craft beautiful, custom-designed books that tell your family's unique story.

Preserve Your Heritage in Print

A Family History Book is more than just a collection of names and dates; it's a cherished heirloom that brings your ancestors' stories to life for current and future generations. Our service helps you to:

  • Showcase Your Research: Present your genealogical findings in an engaging and accessible format.
  • Integrate Rich Content: Combine narratives, photographs, scanned documents, maps, and family trees.
  • Tell Compelling Stories: Weave together factual data with historical context to create captivating ancestral narratives.
  • Create a Lasting Legacy: Produce a tangible book that can be passed down through your family.
  • Share with Relatives: Easily share your discoveries with family members near and far.

We work with you to transform your hard-earned research into a professionally designed and written volume that honors your family's journey.

Our Book Creation Process

From initial concept to finished product, we guide you every step of the way:

Consultation & Planning

We discuss your vision for the book, review your existing research and materials, and outline the scope and style.

Content Organization & Writing

We help organize your information, write engaging narratives, or edit your existing text to ensure clarity and flow.

Design & Layout

Our design process focuses on creating an attractive, easy-to-read layout that incorporates your photos, documents, and charts seamlessly.

Review & Production

You'll have opportunities to review and approve proofs before we manage the printing and binding process to your specifications.

Our collaborative approach ensures your family history book is a true reflection of your heritage and a source of pride for years to come.
